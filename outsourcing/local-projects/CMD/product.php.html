What is CMD?
CMD is a health-check system that combines ancient Chinese medical knowledge with advanced computer technology. It has been used successfully by Russia's cosmonauts for self-monitoring of important life functions in order to prevent illness during long space journeys. This revolutionary system is now available in two versions: PROGNOS, for members of the medical profession, and CMD Computer Medical Doctor, for use by individuals at home or in the workplace.

With CMD computerized diagnostics it can be shown that many of the ancient beliefs of traditional Chinese medicine are still valid today. The combination of proven medical knowledge handed down through centuries and a modern high-tech computerized databank provides optimal results in recognizing and preventing health deficiencies. Although many traditional medical schools still fail to recognize this fact, every sickness has an underlying energy deficit or excess in the related cells. CMD can detect warning signs before a serious health problem develops, and, by restoring this energy to its normal state, can help to prevent illness.

Since 1999, doctors and non-medical practitioners in Western Europe have been using PROGNOS as a complementary medical diagnostics tool. Now a home version has been developed to help provide health management to employees of commercial enterprises and the members of private households with access to the Internet. This new version has been named CMD (Computer Medical Doctor) and its operation is amazingly quick and simple. Energy values are measured at hands and feet with a CMD sensor. These measurements provide information about the general energy levels of the body. Using the Internet, the information obtained by the CMD sensor is sent to the central server located in Frankfurt (Germany). The information is compared with data obtained from more than 12 million previous measurements stored in the server. The evaluation of the information occurs within seconds.
